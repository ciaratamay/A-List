# A-List

///testing build v 0.2 of my customisable multi-shop, shared grocery list app. No logins / sign ups. 
Free to use how you like, see licence for details. I'm not responsible for anything that comes from using this testing build of an app on your device. 
Passwords and lists are not secure currently.

Main Features:
Toggle items I buy repeatedly as opposed to writing out the names of items repeatedly.
Separate lists by shop, without doubling up items on multiple lists.
Quickly copy text lists to share with someone who's in the shop now, or share a list with your household.

Features 07/05/2026

Main menu can create new lists, browse lists you've created before, and import lists if you have the id and password.
There is no sign up or login, you just need a list id and its password to open and share any list.
Main menu also has:
Add / Remove categories (refrigerated, frozen, whatever you like)
Add / Remove shops. Reorder shops in the list by going to edit shops and dragging them.
Create themes for your shopping list app. Use a few pre-loaded or export the template (copies the list of colourable areas and their sample colours to your clipboard) which you can then paste in the box (import template) editing any colours you wish. You can also copy the current active theme's template, and paste it in the box to make minor edits to that theme, and save your own variant, if you like a theme but one or two colours are wrong. 

In the main list: 
Add / Remove items which can have categories and be tagged with one or more or no shops where this item is to be found. Items are visible in any shop tab they're tagged with, and all items are visible in the all shopping tab. 
Toggle on an item you need, pushing it to the top of the list, or toggle it off if you don't need it.
Quick search when adding a new item to a list, if it already exists you will be prompted to create anyway or cancel. (Finds upper or lower case, singular or plural)
Share your list quickly in text form: share any list you're in (all shopping or a specific shop ) by copying to the clipboard and pasting into your messaging app to send someone the list they need to buy from right now while they're in that shop. Only copies toggled on items.
Import a list in text form: this takes the exported text version of a list and tries to update your open list by adding anything that's on it, with any shops or categories too. 

Keep a list on your device locally, or share your list by adding a password, clicking share on the top.  Anyone can access and share - updating across shared versions - as long as they have the unique ID and password you created. 

To do list added - this one just lets you add things to a to do list and check things off. You can copy it and share if you like. I just added this because sometimes when making shopping lists or running errands I also think of things I need to do, or I want to remember errands while also out shopping.

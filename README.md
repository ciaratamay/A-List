# A-List
testing build of my customisable multi-shop, shared grocery list app. No logins / sign ups

Main Purpose:
To create the shopping list app I needed for my family. 
I didn't want to write out and erase the items I buy all the time. having them inactive or active means it's just a toggle. 
I also wanted to have a quick list I could look at when I'm in a specific shop. I buy 00 flour in one shop, chicken in another. Keeping multiple lists is annoying, especially when many items can be found in multiple shops. 
And I wanted to be able to quickly copy and paste my needed items to share with someone who is in the shop now, and can buy for me.

Features 07/05/2026

Add / Remove categories (refrigerated, frozen, whatever you like)
Add / Remove shops 
Add / Remove items which can have categories and be tagged with one or more or no shops where this item is to be found. Items are visible in any shop tab they're tagged with, and all items are visible in the all shopping tab. 
Toggle on an item you need, pushing it to the top of the list, or toggle it off if you don't need it.
Quick search when adding a new item to a list, if it already exists you will be prompted to create anyway or cancel. (Finds upper or lower case, singular or plural)
Share your list quickly in text form: share any list you're in (all shopping or a specific shop ) by copying to the clipboard and pasting into your messaging app to send your housemate / partner the list they need to buy from right now while they're in that shop. Only copies toggled on items.

Or share yourlist by adding a password. Anyone can access and share - updating across shared versions - as long as they have the unique ID and password you created.
